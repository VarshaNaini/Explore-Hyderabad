1.Hyderabad Public School,Begumpet

Established as Jagirdar’s College by the Seventh Nizam of Hyderabad – H.E.H. Mir Osman Ali Khan – in 1923, in Begumpet, Hyderabad; the Hyderabad Public School is older than the Indian Republic. Before the Zamindari system was abolished in 1950, the school was exclusively attended by the sons of aristocrats & elites. It was renamed as The Hyderabad Public School in 1951, and it stands strong in the heart of Hyderabad, sprawling over a lush green campus of 122 acres.
